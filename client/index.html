Comments

Comments is a thing I wrote to play around with three.js and WebSockets. It provides you an uninteresting infinite world and the ability to leave text comments anywhere in the world.

This game isn't "proper" multiplayer: you can't see other players and you can't see comments as they're made.

When you leave a comment, the server will store: your nickname, the comment text, your IP address, and your browser user-agent.

I don't want to remove any comments, but please be respectful and don't post anything illegal. I can't be arsed to moderate a dumb thing I wrote to screw around.

If the server crashes, it will restart after a few seconds. Refresh the page if you get a connection error.
